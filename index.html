Summary

I'm a commercially minded analyst with three years in analytics, built on five years across e-commerce and commercial trading roles in the travel, retail and digital sectors. My work sits at the intersection of data, commercial performance and decision-making, helping businesses turn complex information into clear insight that drives revenue, profitability and growth. I specialise in Power BI, SQL, Excel, forecasting and data modelling, with a strong focus on making data accessible and actionable.

Most recently, I worked at Manchester Airports Group within its digital travel services division, CAVU, where I owned insight and reporting across a business area generating around £70m in EBITDA. Working closely with commercial, finance and marketing teams, I delivered analysis across digital performance, pricing strategy, forecasting and financial reporting to support business decisions and identify growth opportunities.

My career began in commercial trading before moving into analytics, giving me a strong understanding of the commercial drivers behind business performance. Rather than simply reporting what happened, I focus on understanding why it happened and what actions should come next.

Originally from the UK, I recently relocated to Sydney and am currently exploring opportunities across Australia where I can deliver value from day one. Available for immediate employment.
