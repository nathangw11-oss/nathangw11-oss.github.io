Project Write-up

E-Commerce Analytics Suite

A selection of reports and dashboards built to support eCommerce trading, business development and website performance monitoring. Turning complex, multi-source data into clear, actionable insight.

Power BI DAX SQL BigQuery GA4 eCommerce Conversion Rate Funnel Analysis Marketing Analytics Business Development
01

Trading Dashboards Dashboard

eCommerce Trading Dashboard

A multi-tab trading dashboard used daily by the eCommerce team to monitor bookings, income, average transaction value and conversion rate, with dynamic prior year and budget comparisons built in. Each tab allowed the team to drill down into performance by market, product mix and stay duration, giving a much clearer read on where trading was performing well and where it needed attention.

02

Supplier Snapshot — Partner Performance Report

Supplier Snapshot — Partner Performance Report

Built for the Business Development team to share performance data directly with key partners. The report surfaced estimated revenue, booking count, market share, average transaction value and stay duration mix, all filterable by platform, airport and product. It gave partners a clean, professional view of their own performance without needing access to internal systems.

03

Booking Funnel Report

Booking Funnel Report

Built by extracting GA4 data using SQL from BigQuery to track every stage of the purchase funnel on the website, from total sessions through to completed bookings. The ability to segment by marketing channel, device, product category and landing page made it a genuinely useful tool for both the Trading Dashboards and marketing teams to review where users were dropping off and why.

04

Conversion Rate Analysis Dashboard

Conversion Rate Analysis Dashboard

A comprehensive conversion rate dashboard tracking CVR performance across marketing channels, devices and markets, with both daily and 7-day rolling average views to smooth out volatility and surface genuine trends. It was built to give the trading and marketing teams a clear read on where conversion was improving, where it was lagging and what was likely driving it.

05

Marketing Channel KPI Report

Marketing Channel KPI Report

A weekly performance report giving a full picture view of trading across every marketing channel including Paid Search, Email, Organic Search, Direct and Affiliates. It was designed as a side-by-side layout with current year actuals alongside prior year variance, so the trading and marketing teams could assess both absolute performance and trend direction in one view.

The report had previously been maintained as an Excel file, updated manually on a daily basis throughout the week. By rebuilding it in Power BI with a SQL backend, it refreshed automatically every day without anyone needing to touch it. This saved the team several hours of manual work each week and removed the risk of human error creeping into the data.

06

Market View Dashboard

Market View Dashboard

A geographic market performance dashboard combining an interactive bubble map with detailed data tables and a bookings trendline. It was built for the distribution team to quickly identify which markets were growing and which needed attention, all within a single, visually intuitive view rather than switching between multiple reports.

The dashboard made use of Power BI's native cross-filtering so that selecting any market bubble on the map instantly filtered the KPI table and bookings trendline down to that market. This gave users a seamless way to explore performance without ever needing to navigate away from the page.
